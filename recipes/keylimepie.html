Key Lime Pie

Description

I don't know what it is about Key Lime Pie but that's the one dessert that I cannot say no to.

Ingredients:

  • Graham Cracker Crust
  • Lime
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Mix Ingredients
  2. Beat Eggs like they owe you money
  3. Preheat Oven
  4. Slam the goods in the oven
  5. Pull out
  6. Enjoy
